How to create an account in our website

1

Find the button

Go to the travel website's homepage. Look for a "Register" button, usually found in the upper right corner of the page or in our situation below this step by stpe guide.

2

Click on the button

Click on the "Register" button. This will open a registration form where you'll need to enter your personal information.

3

Fill your details

Fill in your details: enter your full name, email address, and choose a password. Some websites may also ask for your phone number or date of birth for added security or verification purposes.

4

Verify your account

Verify your account by checking your email. After submitting the registration form, you’ll likely receive a confirmation email with a link to activate your account. Open the email and click on the link to complete verification.

5

That’s all

Once verified, return to the travel website and log in with your new account. You can now access all the features, such as browsing destinations, booking accommodations, and saving travel plans.
